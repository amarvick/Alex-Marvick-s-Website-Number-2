TUTORING

Yep! I'm an authorized tutor.

Prior to my enrollment at Gonzaga University, I used to do community service every summer during high school for local elementary school kids in lower income areas who needed or wanted to get a jump in their studies in math, reading or writing. This was one of my first experiences in assisting a younger demographic, and I've been inspired by other high school teachers to continue doing so since.

During my time at Gonzaga University, I've successfully been able to work with students independently outside the classroom in order to help ensure they understand concepts that were once confusing; particularly in mathematics or other math related subjects. These students include engineers, business majors and even psychology majors. A list of subjects that I work in are listed below:

  • Calculus
  • Statistics
  • Algebra
  • Geometry
  • Trigonometry
  • SAT/ACT Mathematics

...and more.

If you find that you or someone you know are in a pinch to get by in a class, please feel free to get in contact with me and I can give you more information.
